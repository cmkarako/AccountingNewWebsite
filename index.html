Meet our CPA!

Maurilio's expertise is exactly what you need for your books! As a licensed CPA, Maurilio has deep accounting knowledge and 10 years of accounting experience across multiple industries.


Maurilio received his degree in Accounting from Texas Christian University and completed Masters level courses at University of Texas at Dallas to be eligible for the CPA license. As he completed the CPA exams, Maurilio worked as an Accountant for The Medve Group, a property management company with multi-family communities spanning six states. From there he took a position as Senior Staff Accountant at McLane Company, a food distribution company running $50 billion in gross revenue.


During this time, he helped a local Fort Worth business clean up their books and his passion to serve small businesses was born. He started laying the foundation for his own business and MC Accounting and Bookkeeping, LLC was born!


Maurilio strives to provide the greatest service to his clients and stay on top of the accounting industry trends and regulations. From State and Local Tax Compliance to closing journal entries and other period reports, Maurilio's experience has culminated in the ability to dive deep into the accounting of businesses of various sizes across several industries.
